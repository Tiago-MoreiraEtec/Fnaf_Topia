Synopsis

*Five Nights at Freddy's: Sister Location Fan Game* is a fan game based on FNaF Sister Location. The game recreates the unique atmosphere of the series, with new animatronics and settings. The story revolves around an underground facility full of mysteries and dark secrets.

Gameplay

Similar to the original game, the player must use resources such as ventilation and cameras to stay safe, but with new mechanics and animatronics that bring additional and unexpected challenges.
